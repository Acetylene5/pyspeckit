PySpecKit


A spectroscopic toolkit for analysis of astronomical spectra. Targeted at optical, infrared, and radio spectroscopy. While X-ray spectra could easily be supported, there are already targeted and robust spectroscopic toolkits available to the X-ray community that we will not (yet) be attempting to duplicate.
